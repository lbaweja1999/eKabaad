Waste Pick-up

Trash To Cash

Garbage and waste collection is done as per your request to pick-up the waste at your home. Suitable money is rewarded for each Kilogram and type of waste disposed.

Our company executives ring the bell at the scheduled hour to pick the garbage up. They collect the materials and weigh them using a digital weighing scale, and then calculate the sum value of the materials you are selling. Once you verify the amount, they pays you the same, and take away your scrap.

Organic

All food waste, garden waste, manure and rotten meat are classified as organic waste. Over time, organic waste is turned into manure by microorganisms. However, this does not mean that you can dispose them anywhere.

Organic waste in landfills causes the production of methane, so it must never be simply discarded with general waste.

Lets reduce liiter in Delhi-NCR

More than 160 millions bottles and cans are littered in Delhi-NCR. We need your help - by recycling bottles,cans,cartoons & newspaper,you will be helping environment and also earning along the way.

How it works.

  1. Collect : empty drink bottles, cans and cartons that are between 150ml and 3L. Be sure to remove all lids and bottle tops.
  2. Return : them to a collection point. If you have a lot to drop, please visit a depot. Otherwise our Drop’n’Go pods and pop-up collection sites will do the trick.
  3. Claim : your refunds or convert them to credits to access shopping vouchers and extra rewards. (*Payment options vary depending on location)

Key Recycling Benefits

  • Reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators
  • Conserves natural resources such as timber, water and minerals
  • Reduces your landfill waste
  • Prevents pollution by reducing the need to collect new raw materials
  • Helps create jobs in the recycling and manufacturing industries in the India
